The app lets users take a photo of a restaurant bill and instantly receive a clear, structured breakdown of all the items on it.
The bill image is sent to Google Gemini, which extracts the relevant information from the receipt.
After the scan, users can easily select the dishes they ordered, and the app automatically calculates how much they need to pay.
Users can also add a tip as a percentage, and the total updates instantly.
